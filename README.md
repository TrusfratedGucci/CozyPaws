# CozyPaws

CozyPaws is a mobile app designed to help pet owners manage various aspects of their pets' care and well-being.

## Features

### 1. Medical History
- Keep track of your pets' medical history, including past accidents, sicknesses, and medications.

### 2. Vaccinations
- Record your pets' done, due and upcomming vaccinations and allow to add vaccinations.

### 3. Litter Information
- Manage information related to your pets' litter, family history details.

### 4. Reminders
- Set reminders for important pet-related tasks, such as feeding, grooming, and administering medication.

### 5. First Aid
- Access a first aid guide for pets, including information on common emergencies and how to respond.

### 6. Pet Heat Tracker
- Monitor your pets' heat cycle and receive notifications for peak fertility periods.

### 7. Training Tips
- Access training resources and tips for teaching your pets new behaviors and commands.

### 8. Travel Planner
- Plan pet-friendly travel itineraries and find accommodations that welcome pets.

## Getting Started

To get started with the CozyPaws pet care app, follow these steps:
1. Download and install the app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
2. Sign up for an account or log in if you already have one.
3. Explore the various features of the app and start managing your pets' care more efficiently!
